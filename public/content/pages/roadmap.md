## Content

Platform Roadmap Building the Future of Skill-Wanderer — One Module at a Time Our roadmap outlines the key modules we're developing to create a truly independent, community-driven learning platform. Every module is built with passion, transparency, and our learners' needs at heart. Core Platform Modules The foundational pillars that will power the Skill-Wanderer ecosystem 1 📚 In Progress Custom LMS Module Replacing Moodle with a Purpose-Built Learning System Building a fully customized Learning Management System from the ground up to replace our existing Moodle setup. This new LMS is tailored to embrace our philosophy of learning through failure, practical education, and community-driven content — with no limitations imposed by third-party platforms. Interactive Learning Paths Progress Tracking Practical Assessments AI-Assisted Content Certificate Generation Self-Hosted Infrastructure Goal: Full ownership of the learning experience — no vendor lock-in, no compromises on our principles, and complete freedom to innovate. 2 🏠 Planned Portal & Administration Module A Home for Every Member A comprehensive member portal and administration system that gives learners, mentors, and administrators a personalized dashboard to manage their journey. From enrollment and profile management to progress analytics and role-based access — everything in one place. Member Dashboards Role-Based Access Profile Management Enrollment System Progress Analytics Admin Panel Goal: Empower members to own their learning journey with a clean, intuitive portal that respects their privacy and autonomy. 3 💬 Planned Chat & Communication Module Real-Time Connection for Learners & Mentors A built-in chat and communication system to enable real-time conversations between learners, mentors, and community members. Direct messages, group channels, study rooms, and mentor Q&A sessions — all integrated seamlessly into the platform without relying on external tools. Direct Messaging Group Channels Study Rooms Mentor Q&A Sessions Notifications File Sharing Goal: Foster meaningful, instant communication that strengthens the bonds between learners and mentors — keeping everything within our ecosystem. 4 🤝 Planned Community Module Members Helping Members A vibrant community space where learners help each other grow. Discussion forums, knowledge sharing, peer code reviews, project showcases, and collaborative learning challenges. This is where the culture of "community over competition" truly comes alive. Discussion Forums Peer Code Reviews Project Showcases Knowledge Sharing Learning Challenges Reputation System Goal: Build a self-sustaining community where today's learners become tomorrow's mentors, embodying the karma of shared growth. Supporting & Growth Modules Additional modules we're developing alongside the core platform to support operations, partnerships, and sustainability 📧 Contact & Outreach A dedicated contact and outreach module to streamline communication with potential partners, learners, and supporters — making it easy for anyone to reach out and get involved with Skill-Wanderer. Contact Forms Inquiry Management Auto-Responses 📋 Project Management An internal project management tool to coordinate platform development, track feature progress, manage tasks across contributors, and maintain transparency about what we're building and when. Task Boards Sprint Planning Contributor Tracking 💰 Fundraising & Sustainability Tools and integrations to raise funding for the project — from donation campaigns and grant applications to our Social Impact Token model that sustains free education through community trust and shared value. Donation Platform Grant Management Impact Tokens 📊 Analytics & Insights A privacy-respecting analytics module to understand learning patterns, measure platform impact, and make data-driven decisions — all without compromising learner privacy or selling data. Learning Analytics Impact Metrics Privacy-First 🔌 Integration & API Open APIs and integration points that allow the platform modules to work together seamlessly and enable third-party tools to connect where needed — built on our microservices architecture. REST APIs Microservices Webhooks 🛡️ Security & Compliance Ensuring the platform meets the highest standards of security and data protection. From authentication and authorization to GDPR compliance — protecting our community's trust is non-negotiable. Authentication Data Protection GDPR Compliance Built in the Open, Driven by Purpose Every module on this roadmap is developed with the same principles that define Skill-Wanderer: transparency, integrity, and community-first thinking . We don't just build software — we build trust.
