We define success together at the start of our partnership. This could be revenue milestones, user growth, product launches, or other meaningful metrics specific to your business goals. What if my project doesn't succeed? That's the beauty of the Success Sharing Model—due to our low upfront investment structure, you lose significantly less compared to traditional outsourcing. You can learn quickly what works and what doesn't, then pivot fast without being weighed down by sunk costs. We share the risk together, so you're not carrying the burden alone. How experienced are the community members who will work on my project? Our community includes learners at various stages, but projects are structured with mentorship and oversight. Senior practitioners guide the work, ensuring quality while learners gain experience. Is there any upfront cost at all? While we minimize upfront costs significantly compared to traditional outsourcing, some projects may have modest initial fees to cover coordination and infrastructure. We discuss this transparently during our partnership conversations. How long do partnerships typically last? We'd love for partnerships to last years—building deep relationships and growing together over time. However, we understand that it ultimately depends on your project and business needs. Some partnerships are focused on specific product launches, while others evolve into ongoing collaborations. We're flexible and committed to finding what works best for you.
